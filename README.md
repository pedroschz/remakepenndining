# Remake Penn Dining

A petition site asking Penn to end its Bon Appétit contract and self-operate dining.

→ [remakepenndining.org](https://remakepenndining.org)

## Why I built this

Penn charges students a $6,960 meal plan for food served by a contractor with thirteen years of health-code violations, a history of laying off longtime Penn workers, and — as recently as January 2026 — a dead mouse found in canned-food storage.

Every top-ranked Ivy peer self-operates dining. Penn does not. The gap shows up in the food, the labor practices, and the inspection record, and it's the kind of problem that never gets fixed from the inside without students making noise.

I wanted one place where the public record, peer comparisons, and student experiences live together, and where anyone can put their name on a specific ask instead of a vague complaint.
